Doughnut Chart

A Doughnut Chart is essentially a Pie Chart with an area of the centre cut out. It displays data in rings, where each ring represents a data series. If percentages are displayed in data labels, each ring wil total 100%. The image on the left shows a pie chart while the image on the right shows a doughnut chart.
