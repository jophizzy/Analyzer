Pie Chart

A Pie Chart is a special chart that uses "pie slices" to show relative sizes of data. It is a really good way to show relative sizes: it is easy to see which movie types are most liked, and which are least liked, at a glance. Using our pie chart, you would be able to see the relative percentage of toppings and pizzas ordered in slices.
